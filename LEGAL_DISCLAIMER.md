## 3. Local data and AI providers

Job Search Terminal stores runtime data locally in a SQLite database on the user's machine.

When users choose to run AI-powered actions, such as job evaluation, resume generation, company research, writing assistance, or transcription, relevant data may be sent directly from the user's machine to the AI provider they configured.

Supported providers may include OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google Gemini.

Users are responsible for reviewing the terms and privacy policies of their selected AI providers.

## 4. AI-generated content

AI-generated evaluations, resumes, application answers, outreach messages, company research, and interview materials may be incomplete, inaccurate, biased, outdated, or hallucinated.

Users must manually review all AI-generated content before using it.

Do not submit AI-generated content that misrepresents your skills, work history, education, credentials, identity, or eligibility.
