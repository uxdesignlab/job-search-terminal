# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Job Search Terminal is built to help people during a stressful and vulnerable process. Contributors are expected to communicate with basic respect, clarity, and good faith.

Examples of encouraged behavior:

- Be direct without being cruel
- Focus criticism on the work, not the person
- Assume honest intent until proven otherwise
- Help make the project easier to use
- Respect privacy and personal data
- Remember that job seekers may be under real financial pressure

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Sharing private user data
- Posting real resumes or application data without consent
- Encouraging spam, fraud, or misrepresentation
- Repeatedly derailing project discussions

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, block users, or take other reasonable action when behavior harms the project or its users.
