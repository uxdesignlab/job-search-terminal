# Attribution

Job Search Terminal was inspired by [Career-Ops](https://github.com/santifer/career-ops) by Santiago Fernández.

Career-Ops is a powerful AI-powered job-search operating system built around agentic CLI workflows, Claude Code, Gemini CLI, job scanning, scoring, tailored PDF generation, and pipeline tracking.

Job Search Terminal respects that work and gives clear credit.

## Different product direction

Job Search Terminal is not a fork, clone, or rebrand of Career-Ops.

It takes inspiration from the broader idea of giving job seekers better AI-assisted tools, but it follows a different product direction:

- Browser-based dashboard instead of CLI-first workflows
- Guided onboarding instead of file-based setup
- SQLite-backed local application data instead of markdown/YAML/TSV workflows
- In-app AI provider settings for OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google Gemini
- Resume lanes and visual generated document management
- Application tracking with table and Kanban views
- Interview prep with STAR stories and voice practice
- Accessibility-focused product UI

## Shared belief

Both projects share a belief that job seekers deserve better tools, more agency, and less friction.

Job Search Terminal is free for non-commercial use because job seekers should not have to pay another platform just to manage an already exhausting process.
